앱의 방향

이 앱은 특정 사건의 성공과 실패를 단정하거나 미래를 확정하지 않습니다. 사용자가 자신의 반복되는 성향, 강점, 조심해야 할 흐름, 생활 속 보완점을 현실적인 언어로 확인하도록 돕습니다.

중요 안내

본 앱의 분석 결과는 자기이해와 생활 멘토링을 위한 참고자료입니다. 의학적 진단, 법률 자문, 투자 권유 또는 수익 보장을 제공하지 않습니다. 중요한 결정은 충분한 검토와 전문가 상담을 함께 참고하시기 바랍니다.

Birth time is not required
to read your core disposition and larger life rhythm.

Mukgong Life Timing System is a Korean-English life mentoring app that helps you explore your core disposition, daily rhythm, strengths, and caution points without entering a birth time.

Main Features

  • No birth time required
  • No name required
  • No account registration
  • Automatic lunar date conversion from solar birth date
  • Korean and English support
  • Core disposition, relationships, social groups, career direction, wealth pattern, health rhythm, and final mentoring

Purpose of the App

This app does not predict fixed success or failure, nor does it determine your future. It provides practical self-understanding guidance based on recurring patterns, strengths, caution points, and daily life rhythm.

Important Disclaimer

The result is for self-understanding and life mentoring only. It does not provide medical diagnosis, legal advice, investment recommendation, or income guarantee. For important decisions, please review carefully and consult qualified professionals when needed.
